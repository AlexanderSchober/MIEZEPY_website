MIEZEPY

Open source sotware package allowing the reduction of NSE MIEZE datasets.

Compared to classical and resonant NSE methods, the Modulation of IntEnsity with Zero Effort Neutron Spin Echo (in short MIEZE NSE) method allows to be insensitive to the sample induced spin alterations and is therefore advantageouse for measuring magnetic structures.
